Remember My Number
How to Play:

Using the keypad, enter the randomly generated numbers shown in the panel. The numbers should be entered in the correct sequence to move to the next level.

If you forget the sequence, tap the black panel to show the numbers again. However, you'll have to re-enter the numbers from the start of the sequence.
